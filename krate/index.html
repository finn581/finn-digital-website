Built for the people who keep the lights on

Independent c-store operators run on razor-thin margins, paper invoices, and an inbox full of manufacturer program deadlines. Krate is the iPhone app that puts your entire back office in your pocket — from the loading dock to the smoke case.

📷
DSD Invoice Scanner
Scan any Direct Store Delivery invoice with your camera. Vision AI extracts SKUs, quantities, and unit costs in seconds. No typing. No mis-keying.
📈
Scan-Data Autopilot
Submit your weekly scan-data to manufacturer programs automatically. Krate formats the file, logs each submission, and keeps your rebate pipeline moving without the manual export routine.
✅
Compliance Calendar
License renewals, program opt-in windows, FDA tobacco retailer requirements — Krate tracks every deadline and sends push reminders before you miss the window.
💲
Rebate Tracker
See every manufacturer rebate you have in-flight: pending, submitted, and paid. Know exactly where your money is, store by store.
🏢
Multi-Store Ready
Manage multiple locations from a single account. Invoices, compliance calendars, and scan-data are scoped per store so nothing bleeds across locations.
🔒
Private & Secure
Your invoice data and program credentials never leave Firebase's encrypted infrastructure. Scan-data submissions go through your credentials, on your behalf.
