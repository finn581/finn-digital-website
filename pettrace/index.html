Not another lost-pet board

PetTrace is the kit you wish you had the day before something goes wrong. A toxicity scanner that works at the curb. A vision matcher that compares a stray photo to every lost listing nearby. A Claude-powered advisor that builds a time-phased recovery plan in the moment of panic. And the simple, deeply unfussy QR and NFC tags that take any finder straight to your pet's profile.

✿
Plant Toxicity Scan
Snap a leaf at the park. Two-second verdict for dogs and cats — severity, parts, symptoms, first aid. Offline DB plus Claude enrichment.
◎
AI Pet Match
Photograph a stray. We crop, embed, and compare against every lost-pet listing in your geo-radius. Top matches notified in seconds.
✦
Recovery Advisor
Claude builds a step-by-step recovery playbook the moment a pet goes missing — phased by hour, tailored to environment, time of day, and last-seen place.
❒
QR & NFC Tags
Print a QR for the collar, write an NFC chip, slap a sticker on the carrier. Every path leads to a single profile page. Owner contact one tap away.
⌖
Shelter Map
Every nearby shelter and animal-welfare organization in one screen. No phone tree, no guessing — call the closest, file a report, expand the radius.
❍
Sighting Network
Neighbors drop sighting pins around your last-seen marker. Push notifications when a new sighting lands inside your alert radius.
An informational utility, not a veterinary device. PetTrace's plant toxicity readings are designed to inform — always confirm with poison control and your veterinarian. Pet matches and recovery advice support, but do not replace, contacting nearby shelters and your local authorities.

Get PetTrace

PetTrace is free on the App Store. Scan plants at the curb, match a stray to nearby lost listings, build a recovery plan in the moment of panic, and write QR + NFC tags — start protecting your pet today.
