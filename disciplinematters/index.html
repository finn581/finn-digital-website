Not another habit tracker

Discipline Matters is the system you wish you had built when you said you'd become someone different. It is not gamified. It does not shame. It reads as a magazine and runs as a system — six modules, one editorial voice, every day a sealed entry in a ledger.

📖
The Morning Ledger
Set the priority. Write the three·two·one — three gratitudes, two intentions, one commitment. Read the Stoic morning brief. Seal the day.
⚒
The Work
250 exercises. Six progressive programs. 21 House Workouts including signatures (THE TITHE, FORGE, CENTURION, THE RECKONING) and 15 benchmarks. Live Activity rest timer.
⚖
The Stoic Coach
Reads HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep from Apple Health. Computes a recovery score against your 14-day baseline. Generates a two-paragraph Stoic verdict with a recommendation.
🥩
The Table
Protein, hydration, vegetables. Three intelligent meal recommendations a day with grocery lists. Eat what you would feed the man you intend to be.
⏰
The Hour
A discipline alarm with shake-to-dismiss, math, barcode, and squat challenges. Smart-window wake. Six bells. Reliability check.
🌙
The Evening Reckoning
Weigh the morning's promises — kept, partial, or broken. Answer one Stoic reflection. Aim tomorrow's priority. Choose the sleep window. Close the day.
