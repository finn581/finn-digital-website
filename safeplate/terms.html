What SafePlate is

SafePlate is a food-allergy assistance app for families. It scans barcodes, food photographs, and restaurant menus, and reports whether the detected ingredients match allergens you have configured for one or more family members. SafePlate also provides allergy cards in 11 languages, a restaurant finder, EpiPen reminders, and (with PRO) FDA recall alerts and analytics.

NOT MEDICAL ADVICE. SafePlate is an aid, not a medical diagnosis tool. The presence or absence of an allergen flag does not guarantee a product is or is not safe for any individual. Always read the manufacturer’s allergen statement. If a person experiences any allergic reaction, call 911 (or your local emergency number) immediately. SafePlate does not call emergency services on your behalf.

Allergen detection disclaimer

Detection accuracy depends on the quality of the source image, the visibility of the ingredient list, the completeness of the underlying product database, and the manufacturer’s own labeling. Cross-contamination ("may contain") warnings, ingredient changes, regional reformulations, and unannounced recipe revisions can all cause SafePlate to under- or over-flag a product.
