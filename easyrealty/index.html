The whole transaction, in your pocket

EasyRealty is the iOS app that runs an agent's day — pipeline, clients, showings, and the numbers — and closes it with EasySign, an in-app electronic-signature engine built for real-estate contracts. No desktop, no separate signing service, no chasing PDFs.

EasySign — sign contracts the right way

✍️
Guided Multi-Party Signing
Walk buyer, seller, and agent through each field in order. Face ID verifies every signer, and nothing completes until every required signature and initial is in.
🔍
Auto-Detected Fields
Scan or import any contract and EasySign finds every signature, initial, and date block — and tags each one to the right party: buyer, seller, or agent.
🤝
Dual Agency & Co-Buyers
Represent both sides, or route a married couple as co-buyers — EasySign models real-estate roles, not just generic signers.
🔒
Encrypted & Tamper-Evident
Signed documents are stored AES-256 encrypted, with a keyed (HMAC) audit chain. Every completed contract carries a tamper-evident Certificate of Completion.
⚖️
Legally Binding
Electronic signatures under the federal ESIGN Act and state UETA, with logged consent, identity verification, and a full chain-of-custody audit trail.
📱
No Desktop Required
Prepare, route, sign, and certify a purchase agreement end-to-end on an iPhone or iPad — in person at the kitchen table or on the way to closing.

About EasySign & e-signatures. EasySign supports electronic signatures for purchase contracts and related real-estate documents under the ESIGN Act and UETA. It is not a notarization service: documents that must be notarized or recorded (such as deeds) may require a notary and paper recording, and EasySign is not intended for document types excluded from ESIGN/UETA (such as wills, foreclosure or eviction notices, or court orders). EasyRealty is a productivity tool and does not provide legal advice — consult a licensed attorney for legal questions. Market figures, mortgage rates, and reminders are informational only; verify with the issuing source.
