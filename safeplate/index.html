Built for allergy families

SafePlate keeps a profile per child with their specific allergens and severity. Every scan is checked against the right kid — so the cracker that’s safe for your oldest gets flagged the moment your peanut-allergic 5-year-old reaches for it. Emergency features (SOS button, EpiPen tracker, recall alerts) are always free, no subscription required.
