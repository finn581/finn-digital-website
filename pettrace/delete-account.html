Delete your PetTrace account

This permanently removes your account and all associated data. This cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication user record
  • All pet profiles, photos, notes, and tag identifiers
  • Plant scan history
  • Recovery Advisor session logs
  • Tractive credentials (if connected) — the integration is also disconnected on Tractive's side
  • Affiliate click history and crash reports tied to your account

Backups roll off within 60 days. Any in-app Pro purchase remains tied to your Apple ID — if you reinstall, you can Restore Purchases in the new account.
