Tracker integrations & AirTag

PetTrace supports three tracker tiers. Each carries its own disclaimer:

  • Tractive GPS — PetTrace connects to your Tractive account through an unofficial private API. Tractive may change this API at any time, in which case the tracker integration may degrade or fail until we release an update. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Tractive.
  • Generic BLE / iBeacon tags — PetTrace pairs to off-the-shelf Bluetooth Low Energy tags and tracks them within Bluetooth range only. Out-of-range tags will not produce location updates. This is a proximity tool, not a wide-area tracker.
  • Apple AirTag — PetTrace cannot read AirTag location data. Apple restricts AirTag location to its own Find My app. Our "Find with AirTag" feature is solely a deep-link that opens Apple's Find My. We never display, store, or transmit AirTag positions.

The lost-pet safety core (manual lost-flag, last-seen map pin, Find My deep-link, basic backstop monitoring) is free and will remain free. Always-on cloud monitoring at a higher frequency requires Pro.
